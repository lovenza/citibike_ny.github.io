The Pulse of the City

NYC Citi Bike Ridership Patterns in Response to Daily Rhythms and Weather
Analyzed by Ziang Niu, Zihan Zhou, Bowen Xia, Mungyu Kwok

Data Wrangling

We processed over 3 million rides from September 2025, using stratified sampling to create a robust dataset of 100,000 trips merged with NOAA weather data.

Exploratory Analysis

Discover the temporal patterns and user behaviors hidden in the data. See how commuters and casual riders use the city differently.

Statistical Analysis

We quantified the specific impact of temperature and precipitation on ridership using multivariate linear regression models.
